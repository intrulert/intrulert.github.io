What is Intrulert Smart Security?

Intrulert Smart Security is a modern home security system that uses the concept of Internet of Things (IOT) to put in you control of your own safety whenever and wherever you are. With Intrulert Smart Security you get notified immediately there is an intrusion right on your smartphone through a push message or SMS. Upon notification, you take control from there; either call emergency or dismiss alert. In the case no action could was performed by you, the system moves on to call the emergency automatically (based on your configuration).

At the end of the day, your property is safe.

Features

Super-active sentries

These are modular micro-components (watchmen) that are installed at prospective intrusion points. Sentry is a general name given to these components and could be motion sensors, cameras or alarm buzzers.

Secure app

The Intrulert Smart App uses secured HTTPS requests to interact with the Intrulert System. This gives you the assurance that every intrusion will be detected and sent to the phone without tampering from unintended parties.

Modular

This means the system can be upgraded with features as and when the need for them arises. This means, you could start minimum package and upgrade progressively to a maximum package.

Realtime live stream of intrusion

Upon being notified of intrusion, you get to watch a live stream of it happening. In the case its a legitimate entry, you can then disable any alarms.

Fully configurable

You can make custom configurations to every part of the system; setting up deactivation time and reactivation time, setting ultimatum to call emergency when no action has been made during intrusion, setting the emergency numbers and their priority and so on.
